Our Story

Built on Faith, Growing in Grace, Serving with Excellence.

Who We Are

Founded in the heart of Maridadi, KAG Maridadi Church began as a small prayer fellowship with a big vision. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers dedicated to the Great Commission.

Our church is a member of the Kenya Assemblies of God (KAG), following the Pentecostal tradition and emphasizing the power of the Holy Spirit in everyday life. We believe in practical Christianity that transforms families and the community.

Our Core Values

  • Biblical Integrity
  • Passionate Worship
  • Covenant Community
  • Servant Leadership

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered church that impacts the world through the gospel, raising disciples who walk in the fullness of God's purpose and power.

Our Mission

To provide an atmosphere for true worship, equip the saints for ministry, and reach out to the lost with the love of Jesus Christ.
