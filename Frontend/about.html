Who we are

Founded in the heart of Maridadi, KAG Maridadi Church began as a small prayer fellowship with a big vision. Over time, it has grown into a vibrant body of believers committed to discipleship, prayer, evangelism, and practical service.

As part of the Kenya Assemblies of God family, the church stands in the Pentecostal tradition and emphasizes Spirit-led worship, biblical teaching, and transformed daily living.

Core values

Our vision

To be a Christ-centered church that impacts the world through the gospel, raising disciples who walk in the fullness of God's purpose and power.

Our mission

To provide an atmosphere for true worship, equip believers for ministry, and reach the lost with the love of Jesus Christ.
